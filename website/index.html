Credit creation lifecycle

dxMBR traces every liter from treatment through digital asset delivery, ensuring financiers receive auditable value.

  1. Treatment production: membrane line meets quality thresholds.
  2. Data collection: IoT gateways capture flow, uptime, and energy metrics.
  3. NFT issuance: immutable token records verified performance.
  4. Pre-credit quantification: benchmarks translate data into pre-credit units.
  5. Pre-credit sale: investors or insurers purchase future credits.
  6. Credit registry: ledger catalogs obligations and releases.
  7. Smart contract delivery: automated payout to pre-credit holders.
